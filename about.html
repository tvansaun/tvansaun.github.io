About Me

I was born in Fort Myers, Florida where I spent my entire childhood. When I was 18, I moved to Miami to attend the University of Miami. While working on my Bachelor of Music degree, I decided to explore a field that has always sparked my curiosity: software engineering. After just one introduction to programming course, that small spark exploded into a full blown passion. Since then I have graduated with my Bachelor of Music degree with a minor in Computer Science and am nearing the end of my journey towards my Master's degree in Computer Science at Florida International University. Beyond the classroom I am constantly working on personal projects to hone in my coding skills and knowledge of different technologies. I am driven by my thirst to learn new things and my ability to make the world a better place.
